Lists in HTML

Lists are used to group related items together. HTML supports three types of lists: ordered lists, unordered lists, and definition lists.

Steps to bake a cake

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  3. Whisk the eggs,milk, and vanilla extract.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  7. Let the cake cool before frosting.
